ABOUT THE PROGRAM

The program was created for people with an inactive lifestyle, suitable for intense weight loss in the shortest possible time by reducing the daily calorie intake of food or to consolidate the results after undergoing detox programs. This dietary program does not provide for active physical activity, it is advisable to perform morning exercises and walking at a moderate pace.

In striving for harmony, it is important not to neglect the body's needs for the nutrients, vitamins and minerals it needs. Exhausting diets often only have a temporary effect if proper nutrition is not a habit later on. The 900 calorie program is balanced in terms of the ratio of protein, fat and carbohydrates, and a variety of delicious dishes will help you to follow the diet with pleasure. This slimming program is designed for 7-14 days and allows you to lose up to 3-5 kilograms per week. To consolidate the achieved result or subsequent more comfortable weight loss, it is recommended to increase the calorie content to 1200 calories per day.
