About Healthy Foodie Project

Healthy Foodie is a brand that combines the values of healthy eating with the standards of gourmet gastronomy. We produce and deliver for you the proper nutrition rations, consisting of signature restaurant-level dishes, balanced in calorie content and the composition of the main nutrients.

Created by professional chefs and elaborated in detail by our nutritionists, rations reflect the gastronomic ideas of professionals about healthy food cuisine - as a cuisine that is not only healthy, high-quality and balanced, but also tasty, refined and varied.

We adhere to the principles of continuous self-improvement. Quality is monitored daily, the menu is updated monthly, and the latest innovations and technologies are introduced in the kitchen.
